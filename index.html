"7 DAYS ITINERARY FOR UJJAIN & OMKARESHWAR TRIP"

  • DAY 1:

  • 2023-08-06

    1. Arrival in Ujjain

      • Arrive in Ujjain and check-in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local surroundings.


  • DAY 2:

  • 2023-08-07

    1. Mahakaleshwar Temple

      • Visit the Mahakaleshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva. Experience the divine atmosphere and participate in the morning aarti.

    2. Kal Bhairav Temple

      • Head to the Kal Bhairav Temple, dedicated to the fearsome form of Lord Shiva. Seek blessings and explore the temple complex.

    3. Ram Ghat

      • Enjoy a serene evening at Ram Ghat, situated on the banks of the Shipra River. Witness the Ganga Aarti and take a boat ride along the river.

  • DAY 3:

  • 2023-08-08

    1. Kumbheshwar Temple

      • Visit the Kumbheshwar Temple, known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings. Take part in the temple rituals and soak in the spiritual atmosphere.

    2. Siddhavat Tree

      • Explore the ancient Siddhavat Tree, believed to fulfill wishes. Take a stroll in the serene surroundings and spend some peaceful time under the sacred tree.

    3. Bade Ganeshji Ka Mandir

      • Pay a visit to the Bade Ganeshji Ka Mandir, which houses one of the largest idols of Lord Ganesh. Seek blessings and admire the intricate artwork.

  • DAY 4:

  • 2023-08-09

    1. Jantar Mantar

      • Explore the Jantar Mantar, an ancient observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II. Observe the astronomical instruments and learn about ancient timekeeping techniques.

    2. Harsiddhi Temple

      • Visit the Harsiddhi Temple, a significant Hindu pilgrimage site. Seek blessings from the Goddess Harsiddhi and admire the beautiful architecture.

    3. Mangalnath Temple

      • Head to the Mangalnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Enjoy the panoramic view of Ujjain from this hilltop temple and witness the evening aarti.

  • DAY 5:

  • 2023-08-10

    1. Travel to Omkareshwar

      • Travel to Omkareshwar, a sacred island on the Narmada River. Check-in to your hotel and spend the day relaxing or exploring the local area.

  • DAY 6:

  • 2023-08-11

    1. Omkareshwar Temple

      • Visit the Omkareshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Explore the temple complex and take part in the evening aarti.li>

    2. Boat Ride

      • Enjoy a boat ride on the Narmada River and witness the scenic beauty surrounding the island. Capture stunning views of the temples and landscapes.

    3. Ahilyabai Ghats

      • Take a stroll along the Ahilyabai Ghats and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere. Sit by the riverbank and immerse yourself in the spiritual ambiance..

  • DAY 7:

  • 2023-08-12

    1. Departure

      • Check-out from your hotel and depart from Omkareshwar with cherished memories of the spiritual journey.
